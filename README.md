## Core Features

### 1. **One-Stop Dashboard** (Real-time monitoring)
- **Daily Metrics**: Revenue, cash, inventory, credit given, margins
- **Business Health Score**: 1-10 pulse check
- **Alerts**: Stock low, GST due, credit overdue, competition detected
- **Forecasts**: 4-week outlook with scenarios (what if demand drops 20%?)
- **Recommendations**: Actionable suggestions based on data

### 2. **Business Focus Guide** (Daily coach)
- **Daily Checklist**: 3-5 priorities (5-15 min to complete)
- **Weekly Deep Dive**: 60-min comprehensive review per business type
- **Monthly Strategy**: Financial health, operations, improvements, goal-setting
- **Smart Nudges**: Reminders when you neglect key areas (inventory, customer collection, etc.)
- **Habit Building**: Streaks, badges, leaderboards to maintain discipline

### 3. **Tax & Compliance Engine** (Real data integration)
- **Real GST Rates**: Updated from gov.in (5-28% based on business type)
- **Real License Costs**: State-specific (retrieved from state gov portals)
- **Automatic Calculations**: Every purchase/sale auto-calculates tax liability
- **Compliance Calendar**: Reminders for GST filing, income tax, professional tax, license renewals
- **Tax Scenarios**: "What if I cross â‚¹20L turnover? What changes?"
- **Penalty Simulator**: Shows consequences of missed deadlines (to drive behavior)

### 4. **Dynamic Market Simulation**
- **Real-World Data**: Market prices from commodity APIs
- **Supply Shocks**: Sudden price changes (monsoon, festival seasons)
- **Demand Volatility**: Customers increase/decrease based on season, competition
- **Competitor Activity**: Simulated competitors adjust pricing, you see impact
- **Seasonal Patterns**: Historical data shows peak/off-season dynamics

### 5. **Decision Support System**
- **What-If Scenarios**: "If I cut prices 10%, revenue +15% but margin drops to 4%"
- **Comparative Analytics**: "You vs category average" (benchmark performance)
- **Smart Recommendations**: Based on your data + peer performance
- **Expert Tips**: Real tips from successful MSME owners (anonymized)

### 6. **Profitability Goals & Milestones** (NEW)
- **Auto-Generated Goals**: Based on business type, location, starting capital
- **Key Targets**: Break-even, profitability %, loan repayment, customer count
- **Progressive Milestones**: Month 1 â†’ 3 â†’ 6 â†’ 12 progressively harder targets
- **Real Business Benchmarks**: Compare targets vs actual MSME data
- **Goal Tracking Dashboard**: Visual progress on each goal
